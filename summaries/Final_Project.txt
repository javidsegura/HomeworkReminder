The final project involves creating an AI-powered menu management system that processes restaurant menu PDFs and stores the data. It aims to enhance understanding of database design, ETL processes, and integration with AI. Key learning objectives include designing a normalized database schema, creating an ETL pipeline, implementing data validation, writing optimized queries, and documenting design decisions. The project is due on December 5, 2024, and is worth a maximum of 100 points, with one attempt allowed.
