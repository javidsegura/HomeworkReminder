**Milestone 3: Final Presentation and Demo Summary**

In this milestone, students are required to present their system, showcasing both backend and frontend functionalities in a live demo, with the frontend accessible to the professor. Additionally, students should demonstrate how Azure components manage expense data and, optionally, how the Dead Letter Queue handles invalid requests. 

The presentation must be in .pptx format, limited to 10 slides, and include any relevant scripts or notebooks uploaded to Blackboard. If using GitHub, repositories must remain private. 

Grading will focus on frontend design (15%), backend demo clarity (20%), and presentation skills (10%). The assessment is due on December 8, 2024.
